SEVENTEEN

Seventeen is a 13-member K-pop group, debuted in 2015. The group as a whole is made up of 13 members and are broken up into three sub units- Hip Hop unit, Vocal unit,Performance unit, so that each member has an area of expertise. Fans of this band are called Carats which was inspired from the song called "Shining Diamonds",indicating that they consider their fans as diamonds. Thye are involved in writing and producing a lot of their music.
